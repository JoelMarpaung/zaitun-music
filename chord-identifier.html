Common questions

How does the chord identifier work?

It listens through your microphone, turns the sound into frequency data, identifies likely notes, and matches those notes to known chord formulas.

What instruments does it work with?

Any pitched instrument can work. It is most accurate with one instrument playing a clear sustained chord in a quiet room.

Does it record my audio?

No. The chord identifier uses the Web Audio API locally in your browser and does not upload microphone audio.

How many chords can it recognize?

It checks common major, minor, seventh, suspended, diminished, augmented, sixth, ninth, and added-note chord types across all keys.

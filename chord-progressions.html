Frequently Asked Questions

What is a chord progression?

A chord progression is a sequence of chords played in order. Common progressions like I-V-vi-IV are the foundation of many songs.

What are diatonic chords?

Diatonic chords are the seven chords naturally formed from a major or minor scale. They usually sound good together in the same key.

What is the most common progression?

The I-V-vi-IV progression is one of the most common patterns in pop, rock, worship, and many other styles.

How do I use this progression builder?

Select a key, click diatonic chords to build your sequence, use presets for common patterns, then play or copy the result.
